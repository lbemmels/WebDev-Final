Lindsey Bemmels

I'm a Graphic Designer based out of Minneapolis, MN.

After earning a Bachelor's in Graphic Design from the University of Minnesota in 2005 I headed west, living in Portland, OR and working for a small local print shop. Upon returning to Minnesota I found work as an in-house designer first for Learner's Digest International and currently I am the in-house designer for PreferredOne, a Minnesota based health insurance company.

Professional by day, student by night. I am currently pursuing a Post Baccalaureate in Graphic Design from MCAD (Minneapolis College of Art and Design). If I'm not designing you might find me at a concert or in a movie theater.
